Frequently Asked Questions

How can I list my company in the IAIF Web Catalog?

To list your company, click on the "Register Your Company" button on our homepage or contact our support team. We'll guide you through the registration process and help you create a compelling company profile.

Is there a fee to list my company in the catalog?

We offer both free and premium listing options. The basic listing is free of charge, while premium listings offer additional features such as enhanced visibility, detailed product showcases, and priority placement in search results.

How long does it take for my company listing to be approved?

Standard listings are typically approved within 2-3 business days. Premium listings may require additional verification and can take up to 5 business days. We'll keep you updated on the status of your application throughout the process.

Can I update my company information after it's been published?

Yes, you can update your company information at any time through your account dashboard. Changes will be reviewed by our team and typically published within 24 hours.
